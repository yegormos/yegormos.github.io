A Challenge For The Worthy

Try our little challenge yourself! Use your phone camera and try to film a short video with a couple of your friends on how to make YOUR favorite TEA RECIPE. You have 30 MINUTES to make a 60-120 SECOND VIDEO where you show how to make that special tea. You could show different water boiling techniques, add some fruits to your tea or show some crazy mugs that you have. Don't be shy and try it. You could SEND your VIDEO in to the EMAIL mentioned on the CONTACT PAGE. We are excited to see what you make!
